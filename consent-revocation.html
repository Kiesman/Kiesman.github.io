Consent Settings

This page allows you to manage your advertising consent preferences for Token Stack{R}, as required under GDPR and ePrivacy regulations.

Opt Out of Personalized Ads

Ads in Token Stack{R} are served by Google AdMob. You can opt out of personalized advertising at any time:

Android:
1. Open your device Settings
2. Go to Privacy → Ads
3. Tap Delete advertising ID or toggle Opt out of Ads Personalization
iOS:
1. Open your device Settings
2. Go to Privacy & Security → Apple Advertising
3. Toggle Personalized Ads off

Manage Your Google Ad Settings

You can also manage your ad preferences directly through Google:

  • Google Ad Settings — control ad personalization
  • Google My Activity — view and delete activity data

Remove Ads Entirely

Token Stack{R} offers a one-time in-app purchase to remove all ads. When ads are removed, no ad-related data collection occurs. You can access this option from the Settings menu in the app.
